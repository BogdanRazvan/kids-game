## Spoken instructions (public/audio/)

Rendered offline with **Piper** (MIT) using the Romanian voice **"mihai"
(medium)** from [rhasspy/piper-voices](https://huggingface.co/rhasspy/piper-voices).
That voice's training dataset is **CC0**, so the generated clips are free to use
and commercialize. Letter/number clips are original voice recordings by the app
author.

## Instrument sounds (public/instruments/, scripts/assets/instruments/)

Every instrument note (piano, xylophone, flute, piccolo, bassoon, drum kit,
gong, plus accordion/banjo/bell for the Music game) is rendered from the
**FluidR3_GM** soundfont by **Frank Wen**, licensed **MIT**. Free for commercial
use.

## Emoji

The UI uses Unicode emoji rendered by the device's own emoji font (Apple, Google
Noto, Microsoft, etc.). No emoji font is bundled, so there is no license to
carry — but do not extract/ship a platform's emoji artwork (e.g. Apple's) as
images.
