## Instrument sounds

Single notes (C4) from the **FluidR3_GM** soundfont by Frank Wen, via
[gleitz/midi-js-soundfonts](https://github.com/gleitz/midi-js-soundfonts)
(free to use). Instruments: piano, guitar, trumpet, violin, flute, saxophone.
